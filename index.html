About Us

Project Herzl began as a small Hasbara initiative with a simple but profound goal: to educate people about the complexities of the Israeli-Palestinian conflict. What started as a collection of arguments, covering events from ancient history to the latest developments, has grown into a comprehensive platform aimed at revealing the truth and fostering understanding.

At the core of our mission is a commitment to factual accuracy. We provide in-depth information on significant historical events, such as the October 7th attacks, alongside broader context about Israel's history, struggles, and geopolitical challenges. From ancient disputes to the intricacies of modern-day regional dynamics involving key players like Iran, we focus on offering a nuanced, clear, and evidence-based perspective.

As we grew, so did our ambitions. Creating a non-profit website became the next step in our journey. This space was designed not just as a repository for information but as a dynamic hub for learning and discussion. Through our forums, we encourage open debates and provide a safe space for people to ask questions, exchange ideas, and challenge misinformation. To complement this, we introduced a media section featuring authentic footage: videos and images that allow people to see the reality of this conflict for themselves. These resources aim to shed light on the injustices that arise from false narratives and to give the world an unfiltered view of the truth.

At Project Herzl, we believe that understanding begins with access to accurate information. Our mission is to combat the spread of distorted narratives by equipping the public with verifiable facts and promoting transparency. By doing so, we aim to ensure that future generations inherit a historical record that reflects reality, not bias or manipulation.

Ultimately, our goal is twofold: to educate and to inspire. We want to spread the truth to as many people as possible, creating a platform where they feel empowered to explore, learn, and debate. We also want to honor the memories of those impacted by events like the October 7th attacks, ensuring that their stories are not forgotten.

Thank you for joining us on this journey. Together, we can stand for truth, foster understanding, and build a future rooted in knowledge and compassion.
