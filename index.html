About Us

Project Herzl was born from an intense debate that began in the days following the October 7th Hamas-led massacre.
As we struggled to understand the events, we found ourselves diving deep into the history and the complex narratives that shape the region.
The more we learned, the more we realized how necessary a library that cumulates all of our knowledge is.

What started as a passionate discussion among friends quickly became a mission.
We envisioned a platform that could serve as a reliable resource for anyone engaged in these critical conversations.
So, we created Project Herzl - a curated library of footage, sources, and arguments designed to help people navigate the complexities of the Arab-Israeli and advocate for the safety of the Jewish people in their homeland.
